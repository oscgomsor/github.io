A BIT OF HISTORY

Once the symbol of athletics, sneakers have transcended their primary function to become objects of fashion and commercial desire. From sportswear and street style to runway fashion, sneakers have made their mark as cultural assets.

The last decade has seen a huge change in the way these sneakers are worn. Donning a pair to the workplace or more formal occasions is no longer frowned upon. Even Debrett's, a British firm of etiquette specialists, have given their stamp of approval, deeming them socially acceptable for smart-casual occasions.
